About

CESLeA (Communication, Empathy Sharing, Leading and Autonomy) is an intelligent travel agent that can have a conversion between users that that have plans to travel to any part of the world to guide. It can guides users on travel places that are best for travelling to from booking a flight to suggesting cheaper hotels for one to book for the time of stay.
